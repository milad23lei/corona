The number of people with coronary heart disease in Khatam tripled

06\08\1399
16:04

Javad Mirbehboudi stated in an interview with Mehr: In a situation where since the beginning of the corona outbreak until now, the daily number of people with coronary heart disease in Khatam was less than 10 people, only yesterday, the corona test of 31 people was positive.

"We are more concerned than people who are corona positive and do not observe home quarantine without any signs or symptoms, and are out of the house and moving around the community," he said.

Mirbehboudi said: I ask all my fellow citizens not to leave their homes to protect the health of their families and fellow citizens so that we can prevent and control the epidemic of this disease.

The governor of Khatam also pointed to the lack of oxygen in Khatam Hospital and said: The benefactors of the industrial and mining units of the city provided oxygen and upgraded the oxygen generator of Ayatollah Khatami Hospital in Herat for the well-being of coronary patients, which is commendable.

According to Mehr, Khatam city with a population of about 38,000 people is located in the south of Yazd province and at a distance of 245 km from the center of the province.
